Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about upgrading your intercom.

How does Yama work?

Yama provides a phone number for your building. You program your existing intercom to call this number. When a visitor calls, Yama intercepts it and routes it to the correct tenant based on your settings using cloud technology.

What can Yama control?

Yama primarily controls your building's main vestibule door via the existing intercom connection. Additionally, it can integrate with compatible smart locks to control individual unit doors, maintenance rooms, and common areas.

Do I need to install new hardware?

No new hardware is required for the main functionality. Yama works with your existing telephone-entry intercom system. You simply need to verify that your current system can dial out to a standard phone number.

Do tenants need an app?

No app is required. Tenants receive a standard phone call and can press a key (like '9') to unlock the door.

Do I need an internet line?

Yama relies on your building's existing phone line to receive the call from the intercom. As long as that phone line is active, the call reaches our cloud servers. Our infrastructure is built on high-availability cloud providers.

Can tenants use mobile or long-distance numbers?

Yes! Yama works with any phone number, including mobile phones and long-distance numbers. Visitors dial the intercom, and it connects to your tenant's chosen device, anywhere in the world, at no extra cost.
